### Want to join the group?

There are currently no open paid positions for postdocs or PhD students.

Our group welcomes finishing-up MSc students for 6 month master's thesis projects, if they come with their own funding. However, there may be funding available to continue from a successfully completed master's thesis project to PhD.

Also, if you are interested in applying for funding to do a postdoc with me, please contact me; potential funding sources include various Finnish foundations as well as the European Marie Skłodowska-Curie fellowships.
